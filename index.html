Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you cover for in-person tuition?
We primarily offer in-person tuition in Accra. For locations outside Accra, we can arrange online sessions or special arrangements may be made for areas within reasonable distance.
How do online tutoring sessions work?
Online sessions are conducted via Zoom, Google Meet, or WhatsApp video. We use digital whiteboards, screen sharing, and collaborative tools to ensure an interactive learning experience similar to in-person sessions.
What is the typical process for a data analysis project?
Our process includes: 1) Initial consultation to understand your needs, 2) Data collection and cleaning, 3) Analysis and modeling, 4) Visualization and dashboard creation, 5) Presentation of results and insights, and 6) Ongoing support if needed.
What tools do you use for data analysis?
We utilize a range of industry-standard tools, including Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn), R, SQL, Excel, Streamlit, Tableau, Power BI, and specialized statistical software. The specific tools depend on your project requirements and preferences.
Do you provide materials for tuition sessions?
Yes, we provide customized learning materials, practice problems, and mock tests tailored to each student's needs and exam requirements. All materials are included in the tuition fee.
How quickly can you start a data analysis project?
After the initial consultation and agreement on scope, we can typically begin within 1-3 business days, depending on the complexity of the project and data availability.
